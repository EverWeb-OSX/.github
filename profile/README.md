## 🖌 Overview  
EverWeb Advanced is a visual website builder that lets anyone create professional, responsive websites without writing code. The drag-and-drop canvas gives you pixel-perfect control over every element on every page.

Hundreds of professionally designed templates cover a wide range of industries and styles. Start from any template, customize fonts, colors, images, and layout, and publish a fully functional website in hours rather than days.

Built-in SEO tools, contact forms, image galleries, and blog functionality mean you rarely need third-party integrations. Everything required for a complete website is available within the app itself.

Responsive design tools let you preview and adjust your site at different screen sizes, ensuring every visitor gets a great experience regardless of their device.

For individuals, small businesses, and creative professionals who want a beautiful website without the cost or complexity of hiring a developer, EverWeb Advanced is the publishing tool that makes it possible.

## 🚀 Why Choose EverWeb Advanced?

EverWeb Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets EverWeb Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
